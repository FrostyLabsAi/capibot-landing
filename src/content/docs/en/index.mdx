# CapiBot Documentation

Welcome to the CapiBot documentation. This guide will help you understand and use CapiBot's AI agent orchestration platform through Mission Control, Telegram, WhatsApp, and Slack.

## What is CapiBot?

CapiBot is an AI agent orchestration platform that lets you run intelligent AI agents to automate tasks, manage projects, and even run autonomous companies. Think of it as your AI workforce that works through:

- **Mission Control** — A web dashboard where you manage everything
- **Telegram** — Chat with agents on your phone
- **WhatsApp** — Business messaging integration
- **Slack** — Team collaboration with AI agents
